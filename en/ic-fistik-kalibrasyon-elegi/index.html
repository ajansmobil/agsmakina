Pistachio Calibration Sieve

The Pistachio Calibration Sieve offers high efficiency and quality in pistachio processing. This machine ensures the homogeneous and perfect calibration of pistachios. Made from durable materials, it is long-lasting and suitable for intensive use. With a 0.37 KW motor, it can operate at high capacity. It is easy to use, simple to clean, and reduces operating costs. The pistachio calibration sieve is an excellent choice for pistachio processing plants, food processing factories, and large-scale production facilities.
